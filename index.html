My name is Chris, and I'm a creative Front-End Developer.

I live my life believing that every one of us here on Earth has the unique and real ability to create something amazing.

I believe that it's our responsibility as individuals to tap into that ability.

For me, this is manifested through web development and design. I am truly passionate about my work, and I do whatever I can to kindle and stoke that fire.

More than anything, I try to perceive the world through whole-brain thinking, finding the interconnectedness between the analytical and holistic.

Aside from work, I enjoy getting outside and being active. I'm instensely curious about the world, so I like exploring new places.

I also enjoy hanging out with our 20 pound fur-child cat named Scott who apparently taught himself yoga recently.

If you want to know more about me, don't be shy. I would love to grab a coffee some time and talk web.

Thanks for visiting my page!
